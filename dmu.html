Why DMU College of Osteopathic Medicine is the Perfect Fit

Des Moines University is the number one choice for my medical education for a variety of exciting reasons.

I would like to volunteer in this capacity as a physician and I am excited about the opportunities at DMU with the Osteopathic Finish Line and the Drake Clinic
